About Me

I believe that I can achieve this dream by first going to the college I want. I believe that I can achieve this goal by going to the workforce I like. Even though I am a freshman at Dublin High School (DHS), I believe that I have many skills that I can place on the table. Last year, I tutored two young 6th graders. I used the skills I learned from mentoring the students in order to be able to help others, not just in school work, but in life lessons as well.

I like many subjects, such as, math, science, marine biology, aeronautical engineering, physics, and robotics. I am currently in Dublin High’s Vex Robotics competition team, 5327 B. With my teammates, we are building, and coding a robot that will be able to flip caps, hit flags with a ball, and place caps on a pole. This a challenging project, since we have limitations such as metal amount, defined maximum amount of motors at eight, and finally, a new coding software that we are recently learning. Since I am a freshman, I believe that I need to learn and understand Vex Robotics by discussing it with the upperclassmen. This will help we shape my path in robotics. With the help of Mr. Kaehms, the teacher in charge of the Envirotech Club, and the many lessons I learn from that club, I believe that I can create a robot that can fulfil my expectations for my science fair project this year.
