Type: Scratch

The Spell of Battle

Objective: The Objective of the game is to go to the last level and defeat the boss. This can be achieved by touching the villain and then going to the oposite side of the map.

For this Scratch game, I needed to retrace my coding skills. Even though I had knowledge of this language, I have not used it in many years, bringing me back to square one. With my partner, Diego van Schie, I learned many things such as how to randomize the location of my character. I also learned the value of teamwork. Even though we only had the instructions of building a multi-level project, we worked together creating a fun game. We however, had many ideas beforehand, we decided to choose a game about a wizard that is fighting another wizard that is evil. The main character's powers slowly progress, leading to him having a "warping power", that allowed him to teleport around the map, a lazer that will move to the location of the mouse. From each level, the character will gain one power at a time, making the level more difficult. At the end, we learned many values such as coding skills, teamwork, and the power of documentation.

Learn More

Use "W A S D " to move. Use "E" to warp and it will go to the location of your mouse. You use the space bar to shoot your green lazer, and it will go to the location of your mouse as well. Finally, do not get hit by the red lazers!
